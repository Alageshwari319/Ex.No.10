## Aim:
To demonstrate how various prompting techniques (query decomposition, decision-making, semantic filtering, etc.) can be employed to create content such as reports, articles, case studies, or creative works like comic books, using ChatGPT or similar models. The objective is to highlight how different prompt structures affect the content's quality, coherence, and structure.

## Procedure:
1.	Introduction to Prompt Patterns: Begin by understanding the following prompt patterns:
○	Query Decomposition: Breaking down complex queries into smaller, actionable parts.
○	Decision Making: Asking the model to choose between options or directions.
○	Answer Engineering: Refining outputs by giving detailed instructions on how to structure or format the answer.
○	Fact Check List: Ensuring the content is factually accurate, especially for reports or case studies.
○	Tail Generation: Extending the content logically to create depth in storytelling or analysis.
○	Menu Actions: Presenting multiple action choices and guiding the AI to select one.
○	Semantic Filter: Applying filters to control the tone, style, and accuracy of the content.
2.	Choosing the Content Type: Decide on the type of content you want to create. This could be:
○	Reports (e.g., industry analysis, sustainability reports, etc.)
○	Case Studies (e.g., business solutions, technological innovations)
○	Articles (e.g., opinion pieces, educational articles)
○	Creative Content (e.g., comic book story, short stories, video scripts)
3.	For example, you could choose a business report on market trends, a case study on a successful startup, or even a story-based prompt like creating a fictional world for a comic.
4.	Creating the Prompts:
○	Start with simple prompts to generate initial content.
○	Gradually refine the prompts, moving toward more complex techniques like decision-making (asking the model to pick one of several options), tail generation (extending the narrative), and semantic filtering (adjusting style or tone).
5.	Generating and Refining Outputs: Use the model to generate initial drafts, and then refine the outputs using iterative adjustments:
○	For a report, prompt the model with basic data, then ask for a deeper analysis and insights.
○	For creative content, refine the initial story idea with specific details on characters, settings, or actions.
6.	Review and Evaluation: After generating content, evaluate the outputs for:
○	Coherence: Is the generated content logical and structured?
○	Creativity/Originality: Is the content engaging and fresh?
○	Accuracy: Is the content factually accurate (for reports and case studies)?
○	Tone and Style: Does the content match the intended tone (formal, creative, educational)?

Test Case Scenario 1: Artificial Intelligence Impact on Healthcare
## Basic Prompt 1:

Write a report of the impact of artificial intelligence in healthcare sector in about 200 words.

## Output:
Report: Impact of Artificial Intelligence in the Healthcare Sector

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming the healthcare sector by improving efficiency, accuracy, and patient outcomes. One of the most significant impacts of AI is in medical diagnosis and imaging. AI-powered systems can analyze X-rays, MRIs, and CT scans faster and often more accurately than humans, enabling early detection of diseases such as cancer, heart conditions, and neurological disorders.

AI also plays a crucial role in personalized medicine. By analyzing patient data, genetics, and medical history, AI helps doctors recommend tailored treatment plans, improving effectiveness and reducing side effects. In drug discovery, AI accelerates research by identifying potential drug candidates, significantly reducing time and cost compared to traditional methods.

Another major contribution is in hospital management and administration. AI-driven chatbots assist patients with appointment scheduling, symptom checking, and follow-up care, reducing the workload of healthcare professionals. Predictive analytics helps hospitals manage resources, forecast patient admissions, and improve emergency response.

Despite its benefits, AI in healthcare faces challenges such as data privacy, ethical concerns, and the need for skilled professionals. However, with proper regulations and responsible implementation, AI has the potential to make healthcare more accessible, affordable, and efficient, ultimately improving the quality of life for millions of people worldwide.

## Refined Prompt:

Write a detailed report on the impact of artificial intelligence in the healthcare sector which includes the modern technologies involved in the medical field, scientists doing collaborative research on medicine with artificial intelligence, and the upcoming new technologies in the medical sector.

## Output:
Impact of Artificial Intelligence in the Healthcare Sector

Artificial Intelligence (AI) has revolutionized the healthcare industry by enhancing diagnostic accuracy, streamlining clinical workflows, and improving patient outcomes. AI algorithms, especially those based on machine learning and deep learning, are widely used to analyze complex medical data such as imaging, genomics, and electronic health records (EHRs) faster and more accurately than traditional methods. In radiology, AI-powered tools can detect abnormalities in X-rays, CT scans, and MRIs with precision comparable to expert radiologists, enabling early disease detection and reducing diagnostic errors.

Modern technologies such as Natural Language Processing (NLP) help automate administrative tasks by extracting meaningful data from physician notes and medical reports, while Robotic Process Automation (RPA) streamlines scheduling and billing processes. Wearable devices integrated with AI continuously monitor vital signs and alert clinicians to anomalies in real time, supporting proactive and preventive healthcare.

Collaborative research between data scientists, clinicians, and medical researchers has accelerated the development of AI-guided drug discovery and personalized medicine. AI models analyze vast molecular datasets to identify promising compounds, predict drug-target interactions, and optimize clinical trial designs. Research institutions and technology companies are investing heavily in multidisciplinary laboratories where AI experts work alongside geneticists and pharmacologists to develop therapies tailored to individual patients.

Emerging technologies such as digital twins, quantum computing in drug design, robotic surgeries, smart implants, and federated learning are expected to further transform healthcare. These innovations promise faster drug development, highly accurate diagnostics, secure patient-data sharing, and personalized treatment plans, shaping a future where healthcare becomes more predictive, efficient, and patient-centered.

| **Future Technologies** | Not emphasized | Discusses upcoming healthcare innovations |

The refined prompt produces a more detailed, structured, and professional output compared to the basic prompt. By adding specific requirements such as modern technologies, collaborative medical research, and upcoming innovations, the AI generated a richer and more informative report. The refined prompt also improved the coherence, depth, and academic quality of the response. This demonstrates how advanced prompting techniques significantly enhance AI-generated content.

## Test Case Scenario 2: Creative Writing – Science Fiction Story
## Basic Prompt 2:

Create a science fiction story of a man going to the sun and admires the beauty of it in 500 words.

## Output:
The Man Who Walked Toward the Sun

For centuries, humanity feared the Sun not for its beauty, but for its unbearable power. Yet Dr. Arin Kael stood aboard the solar vessel Helios One, watching the blazing sphere grow larger through the reinforced viewport. He was not afraid. He was in awe.

The ship was built with revolutionary solar-shielding technology, a fusion of artificial intelligence, adaptive nanomaterials, and magnetic deflection fields. Guided by an AI companion named LUX, Helios One moved closer to the Sun than any human vessel ever had. Arin had dedicated his life to this moment not to conquer the Sun, but to understand it.

As the ship crossed the solar corona, waves of golden plasma danced like living fire. Solar flares arched across the surface like cosmic brushstrokes painted by the universe itself. Arin felt small, yet deeply connected. This star was the source of all life on Earth, and now he was close enough to witness its true grandeur.

Against all expectations, Arin stepped into the observation chamber where a transparent energy field separated him from destruction. The AI stabilized the field, allowing him to stand beneath the Sun’s light. The Sun hummed with a deep rhythmic resonance, translated by LUX into a beautiful cosmic symphony.

Arin realized that the Sun was not merely a burning star but a living engine of creation. Before leaving, he released nano-probes carrying valuable data that could transform Earth’s energy systems forever.

As Helios One retreated into space, Arin took one final look at the magnificent star and smiled. Humanity had finally learned not just to survive under the Sun, but to admire it.

## Refined Prompt:

Create a detailed science fiction story of an astronaut who explores the universe in a house-sized spacecraft about the size of a football stadium. Include the struggles he faces while crossing stars and black holes, the discovery of new alien life forms, emotional depth, loneliness, survival challenges, and his disbelief at how far he has come in life. Maintain cinematic storytelling with detailed world-building.

## Output:
The House That Crossed the Universe

The house was the size of a football stadium, floating silently through the universe like a drifting city of steel and glass. It was not merely a spacecraft; it was humanity’s final sanctuary among the stars. Inside lived Aren Kael, a lone astronaut who had chosen isolation over extinction.

As Earth disappeared into memory, Aren often stood beneath the giant transparent dome staring into endless galaxies. He could hardly believe how far he had come in life—from a child dreaming beneath Earth’s night sky to a man traveling across the universe itself.

The house moved using a gravity-bending quantum engine capable of folding spacetime. However, the journey was filled with unimaginable dangers. Radiation storms shook the massive structure violently. Rogue asteroids collided with protective shields, leaving scars across the outer walls. Each system failure forced Aren to repair the spacecraft alone while battling exhaustion and fear.

The greatest challenge came when the spacecraft approached a massive black hole. Time itself distorted. Hallways stretched endlessly, clocks malfunctioned, and terrifying illusions haunted Aren’s mind. Using advanced calculations and extraordinary courage, he used the black hole’s gravity to propel the house deeper into unknown regions of space.

Beyond the darkness, the universe revealed wonders beyond human imagination. Strange stars glowed blue and violet, while living planets pulsed with mysterious energy. On one distant world, Aren encountered intelligent beings made entirely of light and sound. They communicated through emotions rather than language, sharing visions of forgotten civilizations and cosmic history.

Despite discovering new life, Aren’s loneliness continued to grow. The gigantic empty halls of the house echoed with silence. Some days he questioned whether his mission had any meaning at all. Yet every new galaxy reminded him that humanity’s greatest achievement was not technology, but the courage to explore the unknown.

Finally, at the edge of the observable universe, Aren stood beneath the dome once again as billions of galaxies shimmered before him like rivers of light. Tears filled his eyes as he whispered softly:

“I never thought I would make it this far.”

The house continued drifting through eternity carrying a man who had crossed stars, survived black holes, and discovered that the universe was far more alive and beautiful than humanity had ever imagined.
